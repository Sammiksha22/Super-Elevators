Project Overview

We successfully installed a passenger elevator at Aarti Hotel, Pune, Maharashtra to enhance accessibility and convenience for guests. Designed for smooth and efficient operation, this modern lift ensures safety, reliability, and energy efficiency.

A Machine Room-Less (MRL) Passenger Elevator is a space-efficient lift system that eliminates the need for a separate machine room, integrating the motor and control system within the elevator shaft. It offers energy efficiency, smooth operation, and lower maintenance costs, making it ideal for hotels, offices, and residential buildings. MRL elevators enhance architectural flexibility while ensuring safety and reliability with modern technologies like IoT monitoring and ARD (Automatic Rescue Device).
